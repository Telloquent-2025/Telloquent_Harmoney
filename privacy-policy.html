Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Information with Transparency and Care

Last Updated: December 2025
Table of Contents
Introduction Consent Definitions Data Collection Data Usage Data Sharing Cookies & Tracking Data Security Your Rights

1. Introduction

Harmoney Finserv ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, disclose, and protect your data when you access our website, mobile platforms, or any services provided by us ("Services"). By using our Services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Policy.

2. Consent

By accessing or using our Services, submitting information, or communicating with us, you provide your consent to the collection and processing of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Important: You may choose not to provide certain information; however, doing so may limit your ability to use specific features of our Services.

3. Definitions

"Personal Data": Any information that can identify you personally, such as your name, contact details, KYC information, or device information.
"Services": All digital and offline offerings provided by Harmoney Finserv, including our website, communications, analytics, and customer support.

4. What Data We Collect

Harmoney Finserv collects only the information necessary to deliver our services effectively and in compliance with applicable regulations.

Important Security Note: We do NOT collect or store any bank account numbers, credit/debit card details, CVV, PINs, net-banking usernames or passwords, UPI IDs or PINs, or any other sensitive financial login information.

4.1 Basic Identification Information

We may collect personal identification details such as your name, date of birth, gender, and PAN, only when required for identity verification, regulatory compliance, or service delivery.

4.2 Contact Information

We may collect your email address, mobile number, and communication address to communicate with you, send updates, and provide support.

4.3 KYC Information (Only When Legally Required)

Where mandated by law or regulatory requirements, we may collect and process KYC documents such as PAN, Aadhaar (masked), or any other officially valid documents. These are stored securely and used solely for compliance.

4.4 Technical & Device Information

When you visit our website or interact with our digital platforms, we automatically collect technical details such as:

IP address
Browser type and version
Device identifiers
Operating system details
Cookie data and website analytics

4.5 Communication & Interaction Data

We may retain information shared through contact forms, email communication, support requests, surveys, or any voluntary information provided by you during your interaction with us.

5. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for legitimate business and service-related purposes, including:

Providing and improving our Services
Responding to queries and providing support
Handling compliance checks and verification
Sending alerts and updates
Enhancing performance and user experience
Ensuring security and fraud prevention
Maintaining legally required records

6. Sharing, Disclosure & Transfer of Data

We may share your data with:

Service providers for operational support
Legal authorities when required by law
Parties involved in business restructuring

All data sharing occurs under strict confidentiality and compliance safeguards. International transfers occur only with proper protection mechanisms in place.

7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and analytics tools to:

Improve website functionality
Enhance user experience
Track usage patterns
Safeguard security

Cookie Management: You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect certain features of our Services.

8. Data Security & Retention

Security Assurance: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Policy or as required by law. After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

Access your personal data
Correct inaccurate information
Request deletion of your data
Withdraw consent
Opt out of marketing communications

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Chief Information Security Officer:

Email: vignesh@harmoneywealth.com

Questions about our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
