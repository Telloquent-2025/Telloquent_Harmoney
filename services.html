Portfolio Management Services (PMS)

We believe in finding the right companies at right price and staying with the company over a long term to create wealth. Buying companies with decent ROE, ROCE with great management available at reasonable valuation to create long term wealth.

Synergy Strategy

  • Identify companies (business) that we can understand, with honest and able management with certain degree of competitive advantage.
  • Evaluate the business with conservative assumptions to arrive at approx intrinsic value of the company and try to buy the company at a discount to the intrinsic value and stay with the company for long run.
  • General approach is to identify great companies which goes through temporary problems and buy them at a discount so that once the problem gets resolved value gets created.

India Story Strategy

Invest in stocks that will belong to one of the 7 themes:

Growth in rural income
Higher savings and investments in financial instruments
High value consumption growth
Growth in working population
Infrastructure investments
Export led growth
Entrepreneurship stroking new high growth sectors

Prospect Strategy

Based on Graham approach of buying companies at bargain price with the below mentioned criteria:

  • Stocks which are trading at below 0.8x time's book value with high dividend yield
  • Debt to equity ratio of the company should not be higher than 1x
  • Companies having revenue of at least Rs 2-3 bn so as to ensure that the company has sufficient staying power
  • Further analysis on stocks' fundamentals and its business model to filter the appropriate companies

Intellect Strategy

This strategy will invest in portfolio of mutual fund DIRECT OPTION like:

  • Equity, debt, liquid, balanced, hybrid or ETF or a combination of above
  • Bonds, Bank deposit and similar products
  • Aim to manage risks under different market situations
  • Allocation of investment within the strategy shall be made with a view to meet the changing investment needs, risk profiles and dynamic market circumstances
