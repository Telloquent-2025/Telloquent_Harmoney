About Harmoney

Assisting you in achieving abundance in wealth leading to financial freedom

Our Story: Inspired by India's Harmon(e)y

India is the best example on this planet for human-kind living in Harmon(e)y. All the different culture, traditions, faith and languages synergizing into one movement - India. Drawing the best of every difference, synthesizing into a Harmonious orchestra, our Dharma enshrines at its heart, a deep sense of respect for all aspects of Creation.

Living in Harmony at peace with ourselves, with nature and with this planet Earth with a defined character of India ness, we are proud to be Indians.

Equally diversified, is the financial instruments market today in India. Different people have different needs. These needs vary from person to person with various criteria of age, income level, family size and so on.

Harmoney strives to match these needs with the variety of financial instruments and recommend an appropriate blend that will ensure Harmony in the lives of such persons we are associated with.

Our Vision for India's Prosperity

When Indians became free, India became a free country. When Indians become wealthy, India will become wealthy. Harmoney dedicates its life to this great cause and in the process make a living for itself.

We believe that true financial freedom comes from achieving harmon(e)y between your aspirations and your financial capabilities. Our approach combines the wisdom of traditional values with modern financial expertise.
