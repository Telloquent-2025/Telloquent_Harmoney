Grievance Redressal Mechanism

For any complaint or dispute regarding service, follow the escalation matrix:

1

Contact Relationship Manager

First point of contact for discussing concerns/issues in detail.

2

Grievance Cell

If no satisfactory response from RM, write to: support@harmoneywealth.com

3

Compliance Officer

If no response within 21 days, contact: ingrcomplaince@harmoneywealth.com
Or write to: Harmoney Finserv India Pvt. Ltd., No: 78 2nd Floor, 11th Main, 14th Cross, 6th Sector, HSR Layout, Bangalore-560102

4

SEBI SCORES

If unsatisfied with resolution, lodge complaint on: scores.gov.in

5

Online Dispute Resolution

For SMART ODR Portal access: SMARTODR Portal
